What Have Others Done?

WWF, or the World Wide Fund for Nature, is a non-governmental organization whose main goal is to promote environmental conservation. Since its foundation in 1961, WWF is the main driving force behind many of the environmental activism and conservation campaigns.

One of the campaigns brought forth by the WWF targets politicians in control of the government, changing their mindset and pressuring them to act before climate change happens catastrophically. To do so, WWF ensured that the goal of keeping greenhouse gas levels within the limits is included in the first international agreement on climate change.
